INGREDIENTS TO PREPARE YAM AND EGG SAUCE
For Yam Egg sauce
Tuber of Yam 4 fresh eggs or more
Salt to taste Fresh tomatoes
Fresh scotch pepper(about half a bowl full)
Some fresh onions
Crayfish, spices,some bouillon cubes and salt to taste
Groundnut oil

HOW TO PREPARE YAM AND EGG SAUCE

  1. Peel the yam and cut into smaller bits.
  2. After that, put them into a clean cooking pot, add some water to it and some salt to taste.
  3. Then, put on fire to cook for about 20-30 mins.
  4. On the other hand, take your fresh eggs. Rinse them and break them into a bowl and whisk till you achieve a good consistency.
  5. Cut your onions, scotch peppers ans tomatoes into little bits unto a bowl.
  6. Add your spices, bouillon cubes and salt to taste to your whisked eggs and also to the sliced pepper and tomatoes.
  7. Take a frying pan, add some groundnut oil to it and allow to heat up.
  8. When it is heated enough, fry your onions ,pepper,tomatoes and even your crayfish for a minute or two.
  9. Then pour in your whisked eggs and stir.
  10. After 5-10 mins, remove your egg sauce from the pan as it is ready.
  11. Don't forget to check on the yam you are boiling. Remove it wwhen it is done.
  12. Serve on a dish and enjoy!
